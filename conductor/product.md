# Initial Concept
AI-driven Personal Account Dealing compliance automation system.

# Product Guide

## Overview
PA Dealing is an AI-driven compliance automation system designed to streamline the Personal Account Dealing (PAD) process. By automating routine requests and risk assessments, it ensures regulatory adherence while significantly reducing the operational burden on compliance teams.

## Target Users
- **Employees:** Submit personal trading requests conveniently via a Slack-based chatbot and receive real-time status updates.
- **Managers:** Review and approve or reject subordinate trading requests through an integrated workflow.
- **Compliance Officers:** Oversee the entire PAD ecosystem, manage complex cases, and monitor for policy breaches via a centralized React dashboard.

## Core Features
- **Slack Request Interface:** A seamless chatbot experience for trading request submission.
- **Automated Risk Engine:** Real-time classification of trades against restricted lists, market activity, and internal policies.
- **Multi-Stage Workflows:** Automated approval routing from managers to compliance with built-in escalation logic.
- **Post-Trade Monitoring:** Automated verification of contract notes and enforcement of minimum holding periods.

## Business Goals
- **Operational Efficiency:** Automate high-volume, low-risk requests to allow Compliance to focus on complex advisory and investigative work.
- **Regulatory Assurance:** Maintain 100% adherence to financial regulations and internal trading policies.
- **Enhanced User Experience:** Provide employees with a fast, transparent, and modern interface for compliance interactions.

## Success Metrics
- **Compliance Burden Reduction:** Measured by the shift in compliance team time from routine processing to complex case management.
- **Processing Velocity:** Significant reduction in the "request-to-decision" cycle time.
- **Policy Integrity:** Zero undetected breaches in audited trading activities.
